Solar & Inverter

Clean, Reliable

End-to-end solar power and inverter solutions — designed, supplied, installed, and maintained for homes, offices, and industrial facilities. Cut energy costs, eliminate downtime, and power what matters, day and night.

Why Solar

Power Without Compromise — Or Diesel

Erratic grid supply and rising fuel costs make conventional power a liability. Visionspec engineers solar and inverter systems sized precisely to your load profile — so the lights stay on, equipment stays protected, and your monthly energy bill stops surprising you.

From a 1.5kVA home backup to multi-megawatt commercial arrays, we handle site assessment, load analysis, panel and inverter selection, battery sizing, installation, and long-term maintenance. Every system is built around tier-1 components and warranted for years of service.

Our Capabilities

Engineered Energy Solutions

Solar System Design & Installation

Custom-sized PV arrays for residential, commercial, and industrial sites — site survey, load profiling, and certified installation by trained technicians.

Inverter Systems & Backup

Pure sine-wave inverters and hybrid systems from leading brands. Seamless changeover, surge protection, and capacity matched to your critical loads.

Battery Storage & Lithium Upgrades

Tubular, gel, and lithium battery banks engineered for deep-cycle endurance. We size for autonomy days that match your usage, not the showroom brochure.

Maintenance & Monitoring

Scheduled servicing, panel cleaning, performance monitoring, and rapid-response repairs. Your system stays at peak output year after year.
